Description

On WildLand Corp. your are a director of a major industry of manufatory and you want to get money in a beautiful wild land. But take care on the nature because if you expand a lot or if your production is very polluting you will be attacked by activists or jaguars and your workers will leave their villages. So make money but take care on your environement!
